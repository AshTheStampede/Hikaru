How the Prototype Thinks

The engine blends legality checks with tactical heuristics, candidate pruning, and shallow lookahead. Lower kyu levels favor safer randomization; stronger ranks do more local reading, value shape and liberties more sharply, and reduce blunders.

Immediate Upgrade Targets

• Replace heuristic search with Monte Carlo tree search plus neural priors.
• Add SGF import/export, time controls, handicap stones, and review mode.
• Improve scoring with full territory resolution, seki awareness, and dead-stone marking.
• Support engine workers for stronger play without freezing the UI.
• Add joseki training, tsumego mode, and online/local multiplayer.
This prototype is tuned to feel playable and modern in a browser first. On large 19×19 boards, dan-level play here is only an approximation of stronger amateur behavior rather than a true dan-calibrated engine.
